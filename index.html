FAQs


How are your classes different from other coaching institutes?

Our classes are led by Dr. Vishal Munde, a PhD-qualified biology expert with over 10 years of teaching experience. We maintain small batch sizes to ensure personalized attention, focus on conceptual understanding rather than memorization, and provide comprehensive study materials developed by Dr. Munde himself.

What is the batch size for NEET coaching classes?

We maintain a maximum batch size of 15-20 students to ensure quality interaction and personalized attention for each student.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials developed by Dr. Munde, including concept notes, practice questions, diagrams, and revision sheets. These materials are regularly updated to reflect the latest exam patterns.

How can I enroll in your coaching program?

You can visit our center in Laxmi Nagar, call us, or fill the inquiry form on this website. We recommend booking a free demo class to experience our teaching methodology before enrollment.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer hybrid learning options with both offline and online classes to accommodate different student needs and preferences.
