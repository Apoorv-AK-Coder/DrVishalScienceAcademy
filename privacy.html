Privacy Policy
Dr. Munde's Biomantra

This privacy policy for coaching institute explains how Dr. Munde BioMantra (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We value your privacy and are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of any personal information you share with us. This coaching center privacy policy applies to information collected through our website, during the admission process, and throughout your educational journey with us.


Information We Collect


Personal Information

At Dr. Munde BioMantra, we collect various types of personal information to provide our educational services effectively:

  1. Basic Details: Name, date of birth, gender, photograph, contact information (phone number, email address, residential address)
  2. Academic Information: Previous academic records, test scores, performance assessments
  3. Parent/Guardian Information: Name, contact details, relationship to the student
  4. Payment Information: Bank details, transaction records, payment history
  5. Attendance Records: Class participation, attendance logs
  6. Performance Data: Test results, progress reports, academic achievements

Our coaching class privacy rules ensure that we only collect information that is necessary for providing quality education and maintaining accurate records as required by educational authorities.


Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically:

  1. Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  2. Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on the website, links clicked
  3. Location Information: General location based on IP address

Cookies and Similar Technologies: Small data files stored on your device (more details in our Cookie Policy section)


How We Use Your Information

Our educational institute data policy ensures that we use your information responsibly and only for legitimate purposes:

  • Educational Services
  • Providing personalized coaching and educational support
  • Tracking academic progress and performance
  • Creating customized study plans and learning materials
  • Communicating important updates about classes, exams, and events
  • Facilitating doubt-clearing sessions and additional support
  • Administrative Purposes
  • Processing admissions and enrollments
  • Managing fee payments and financial records
  • Maintaining attendance records
  • Generating performance reports and certificates
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Communication
  • Sending class schedules and changes
  • Providing information about upcoming events, workshops, and seminars
  • Sharing educational resources and study materials
  • Notifying about new courses or programs
  • Responding to queries and concerns
  • Website Improvement
  • Analyzing website usage to improve user experience
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Developing new features based on user feedback
  • Personalizing content based on user preferences
  • Measuring the effectiveness of our educational content

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We implement strict student data protection measures and only share your information in the following circumstances:


Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Learning management systems
  • Payment processing
  • Website hosting and maintenance
  • Email and communication services
  • Analytics services

These service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to use your information for the specific services they provide to us.


Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law:

  • In response to a court order or legal process
  • To comply with regulatory requirements
  • To protext our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • To investigate potential violations of our terms of service
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activities

Educational Partners

With your consent, we may share limited information with:

  • Educational institutions for admission purposes
  • Examination boards for registration
  • Academic competitions and events organizers
  • Educational research institutions (anonymized data only)

We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.


Data Security

The educational data security measures we employ include:

  • Encryption: All sensitive information is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Access Controls: Only authorized staff members have access to personal information
  • Regular Audits: We conduct periodic security assessments of our systems
  • Staff Training: Our team is trained on privacy and security best practices
  • Physical Security: Our premises have appropriate security measures to protect physical records
  • Secure Disposal: We securely dispose of personal information when no longer needed

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.


Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience:


Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Performance Cookies: Improve website performance and user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our website.


Your Rights and Choices

Under our coaching class privacy rules, you have several rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Correction

  • You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information
  • You may obtain a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used format

Deletion and Restriction

  • You can request deletion of your personal info when it's no longer needed
  • You may restrict the processing of your data under certain circumstances
  • You have the right to withdraw consent for specific processing activities

Communication Preferences

  • You can opt out of receiving marketing communications
  • You can choose your preferred communication channels
  • You may update your contact information at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the “Contact Us” section below.


Children's Privacy

We take student data protection seriously, especially for minors. Our services are primarily directed at students preparing for competitive exams like NEET, typically aged 16 and above. For students under 18 years of age:

  • We collect personal information only with parental/guardian consent
  • Parents/guardians have the right to review information collected about their child
  • Parents/guardians can request deletion of their child's personal information
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.


Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our educational services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Typically, we retain student records for a period of 3 years after completion of the course or as required by applicable laws. Financial records may be retained for longer periods to comply with tax and accounting requirements.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

This coaching center privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting a notice on our website
  • Sending an email to the address we have on file
  • Providing a notification when you next log in to our student portal

The date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last updated. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.


International Data Transfers

While Dr. Munde BioMantra primarily operates in Delhi, India, some of our service providers may be located in other countries. If we transfer your information to countries outside India, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable laws.
